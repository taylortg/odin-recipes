An Ordinary Taco Recipe

An ordinary taco.

My tacos are nothing special, but I do love them. They are simple and easy to cook.

Simplicity can often be overlooked. If the meat is seasoned well and nice fresh produce is used, I assure you the tacos will be delicious.

Ingredients

  • Meat (braised beef, shredded chicken, whatever you like)
  • Fresh tomatoe
  • Avocado
  • Onion
  • Crema

Steps

  1. Cook meat
  2. Chop vegetables
  3. Combine in the right combination
  4. Enjoy
